The Problem with Food

People are less productive if they are unsure of their next meal. This is food insecurity. Healthy food must be distributed quickly while it is fresh.

40% of food is thrown away

1 in 7 people are food insecure

How We Can Do Better

You can post to the community board that you have extra food that can be saved while a community of volunteers nearby are ready to catch your donation! Once a volunteer accepts the challenge they can deliver the saved food to a partner within their preferred route. Everyone will receive notifications to allow as the food is caught, delivered and given to people in your community!

About catchAndDeliver

Many businesses have leftover food at the end of the day. catchAndDeliver lets them post those extras and have a volunteer come pick up the food and take it to a predetermined place of need.
